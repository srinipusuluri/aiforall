üåç Why AI Evolved

Artificial Intelligence evolved from the human desire to make machines think, learn, and act intelligently ‚Äî like us.

It began with rule-based systems (1950s‚Äì1980s), where humans defined logic manually, but these couldn‚Äôt adapt or scale.

Then came machine learning ‚Äî algorithms that learn from data instead of being explicitly programmed.

With the explosion of data and computing power, AI grew fast ‚Äî and deep learning (neural networks) became the engine behind modern AI.

Today, AI continues evolving with foundation models, large language models (LLMs), and multimodal systems that understand text, images, audio, and video together.

The evolution reflects one goal: to augment human potential ‚Äî not replace it.

What you'll learn: Model development, data preprocessing, feature engineering, model training, evaluation, and basic optimization techniques.

Duration: 12-16 weeks | Difficulty: Intermediate-Advanced | Prerequisites: Steps 1-2 and Python proficiency

Mini-Projects to Build:
